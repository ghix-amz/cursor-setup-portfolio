### Insight
This post uses a classic “list-style hook” (“4 easy steps”) to attract attention, but then deliberately breaks expectations by shifting into a deeply personal and emotional story.
This pattern creates a strong contrast between what the reader expects (simple advice) and what they receive (a raw, vulnerable narrative), which increases engagement and retention.
The storytelling element is the core strength of this post. By sharing personal struggles and experiences, the author builds trust and emotional connection, making the message more impactful and memorable.
The post also reframes the concept of success, moving from external validation (money, followers) to internal clarity and purpose. This adds depth and positions the author as a thoughtful and credible voice.
Overall, the combination of a curiosity-driven hook, pattern interruption, and emotional storytelling makes this post highly engaging and effective on LinkedIn.
