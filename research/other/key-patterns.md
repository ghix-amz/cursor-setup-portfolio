# Key Patterns from LinkedIn Experts

## 1. Content is not just creation
Most experts emphasize that content alone is not enough. Distribution and positioning play a critical role in performance.

## 2. Clarity and positioning drive growth
Clear messaging and strong positioning are more important than algorithm hacks.

## 3. Trust > short-term conversion
Building trust through valuable content is more effective than pushing for immediate results.

## 4. Distribution is a competitive advantage
Repurposing and sharing content across multiple channels increases reach and impact.

## 5. Buyer behavior is changing
Modern buyers research independently, often before interacting with sales teams.

## 6. AI is changing discovery
Content must now be visible not only to users, but also to AI systems that summarize and recommend information.
